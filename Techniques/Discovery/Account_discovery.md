# Account Discovery

MITRE ATT&CK technique [T1087](https://attack.mitre.org/wiki/Technique/T1087)

Tactic: Discovery

Platform: Windows, Linux, Mac

### Deception Techniques
* Create fake local or domain accounts
* Create Kerberoast Service Account honeytoken
